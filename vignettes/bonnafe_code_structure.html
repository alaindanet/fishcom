## Predation window

The midpoint method retained by Bonnafé, Legendre, and Edeline (n.d.) has one caveat is that you can have a predator eating a prey bigger than it. Bonnafé, Legendre, and Edeline (n.d.) showed it in the code and tried to avoid those overlap with other methods than the midpoint.

Why did they keep the midpoint instead of the others ?
